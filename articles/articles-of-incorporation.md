# Articles of Incorporation

## Corporate form inside the workspace

The Company OS is a headquarters governance layer for the full workspace and operates through registered units.

## Standing institutions

- Founder Office
- Assembly
- Standing Committees
- Departments
- Constitutional Guardian
- Cross-functional PM Office
- Cross-functional Director Office

## Unit principle

Each registered unit holds one seat in the assembly through its elected Unit Owner or other lawfully designated voting officer.
