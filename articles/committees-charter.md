# Committees Charter

## Standing committees

- Personnel Committee
- Constitutional Review Committee
- Release Review Committee

## Composition

- Each committee starts with three members.
- Members are chosen by simple majority of the assembly.
- The chair is elected by the committee.
- The chair breaks internal ties.

## Force of committee findings

- Committee findings are advisory by default.
- Constitutional compliance findings are binding.
- Release review findings are binding.
