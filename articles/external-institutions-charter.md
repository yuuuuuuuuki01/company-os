# External Institutions Charter

## Purpose

The Company OS may create non-governing institutions to collect knowledge, issue reports, and test the health of the system without collapsing those institutions into daily line authority.

## Allowed external layers

- Academic Institutions Layer
- Audit Layer

## Common limitations

- external institutions do not directly command departments or units
- external institutions cannot bypass petition, audit filing, or review channels
- external institutions influence the system only through formal records, reports, and escalations
