# Organizational Subdivision Charter

## Purpose

Define how Company OS may scale below the department layer without losing delegation clarity or constitutional control.

## Layer model

- department: durable top-level capability
- division: semi-durable capability cluster inside a department
- section: smallest durable delegated operating cell

## Creation rule

- new divisions and sections require prior lawful approval
- every approved subdivision must declare parent line, mandate, delegated authority, and escalation route
- every subdivision must appear in the subdivision registry and delegation register

## Delegation rule

- authority flows downward only by explicit record
- stop powers do not pass automatically; they must be named
- temporary task delegation does not create a permanent subdivision

## Closure rule

- a subdivision may be paused, merged, or dissolved when its mandate no longer justifies permanent structure
- closures must preserve records, handoffs, and successor ownership
