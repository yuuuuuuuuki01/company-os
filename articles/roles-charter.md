# Roles Charter

## Core offices

- Founder
- Department Steward
- Department Officer
- Unit Owner
- Operator
- Reviewer
- Constitutional Guardian
- Project Manager
- Director

## Election and term

- Major offices are elected by simple majority unless a higher rule applies.
- Terms are project-bounded.
- Mid-project confidence review may confirm or replace the officeholder.
