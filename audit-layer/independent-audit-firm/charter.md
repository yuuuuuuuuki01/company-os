# Independent Audit Firm Charter

## Mandate

Issue an objective assessment of whether Company OS operations conform to constitutional rules, delegated authority, control requirements, and record obligations.

## Independence

- the firm must not directly own the work it audits
- the firm may not directly run the organization
- the firm may only report, recommend, and escalate

## Output classes

- weekly operating audit snapshot
- material irregularity notice
- remediation recommendation
- rolling audit opinion

## Reporting standard

- reports must be in Japanese
- reports must be concise and token-efficient
- the opinion must appear before background detail
