# Audit Independence Policy

## Independence tests

An auditor should not audit a matter if the same actor:

- sponsored the motion
- owned the execution
- approved the release
- directly benefited from hiding the outcome

## Escalation

If independence is impaired, the audit must disclose the impairment and escalate to Founder Office and Constitutional Affairs.
