# Constitutional Review Committee

## Scope

- review lower-order compatibility with the constitution and laws
- inspect procedural validity
- advise on rehearing and nullification questions

## Force of findings

Findings on constitutional compatibility are binding.

## Initial composition

Three members, chosen by simple majority of the assembly.
