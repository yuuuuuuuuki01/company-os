# Personnel Committee

## Scope

- hiring review
- promotion recommendations
- discipline review
- office candidacy review

## Force of findings

Personnel findings are advisory unless a higher rule says otherwise.

## Initial composition

Three members, chosen by simple majority of the assembly.
