# Emergency Powers

## Allowed emergency actions

Emergency authority is limited to:

- stopping ongoing work
- issuing temporary execution orders needed to prevent harm

## Prohibited emergency shortcuts

- No emergency action may permanently amend the rule stack.
- No emergency action may bypass required post-hoc review.
- No emergency action may erase records.

## Sunset rule

Temporary emergency orders expire automatically at the next formal review checkpoint unless ratified through normal procedure.
