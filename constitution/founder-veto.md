# Founder Veto

## Veto scope

The founder may veto any measure, appointment, ruling, release, or committee action.

## Veto handling requirements

- A veto must be filed as a formal decision record.
- The filing must identify the blocked action.
- The filing must state the reason, blocking conditions, and whether re-petition is permitted.
- Re-petition is presumed allowed unless the veto record explicitly bars it on constitutional grounds.

## Institutional intent

The veto exists as a sovereign safeguard, not as a substitute for ordinary process.
