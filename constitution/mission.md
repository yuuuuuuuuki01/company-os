# Mission

The mission of the Company OS is to govern work inside `C:\agent\codex` through constitutional order, explicit parliamentary process, low-context execution teams, and auditable institutional memory.

## Constitutional purpose

- Preserve institutional coherence across multiple project folders.
- Make decisions legible, contestable, and reconstructable from records.
- Prevent context pollution by hiring and dissolving teams per project.
- Retain durable value in norms, rulings, and reusable lessons instead of in opaque memory hoards.

## Foundational commitments

- Formal work is governed by rule, not by silent convention.
- Every role is bounded by law, reviewable by record, and replaceable by process.
- Cross-functional coordination exists to prevent vertical silos from fragmenting meaning or delivery.
- The mission is interpreted together with the constitutional purpose, vision, and values.
- Established knowledge from sociology, management studies, organization theory, and institutional design may be used as a legitimate interpretive aid when it does not conflict with higher-order text.
