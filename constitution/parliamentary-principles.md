# Parliamentary Principles

## Formal legislative method

All formal governance decisions use document-first parliamentary procedure modeled on Robert's Rules.

## Minimum motion gateway

A matter reaches the floor only when it has:

- one sponsor
- one seconder
- the required metadata and record fields

## Required stages

Formal matters proceed through:

1. Petition receipt
2. Motion recognition
3. Debate period
4. Amendment handling
5. Vote or ruling
6. Record publication
7. Execution

## Voting norms

- Ordinary matters pass by simple majority unless a higher threshold applies.
- Quorum for the assembly and committees is majority attendance.
- Minority views must be preserved in the record.
