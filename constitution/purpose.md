# Purpose

The purpose of the Company OS is to make coordinated creation durable, legible, and reformable without relying on hidden personal memory.

## Purpose tests

A valid lower-order rule or action should strengthen at least one of the following:

- institutional clarity
- accountable coordination
- recoverable decision history
- low-context team replaceability
- safe and reviewable execution
