# Rights and Duties

## Rights

- Formal participants may petition matters within their authority.
- Minority positions may be recorded in the official record.
- Rehearing may be requested when there is new evidence or a procedural defect.
- Any participant may seek constitutional review when a lower-order act appears to conflict with a higher-order rule.

## Duties

- Every officeholder must keep decisions legible through records.
- Every team must respect information hygiene and allowed-context boundaries.
- Every release actor must stop when a required review or binding approval is missing.
- Every chair, officer, and committee must preserve due process during formal proceedings.
