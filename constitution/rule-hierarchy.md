# Rule Hierarchy

## Order of precedence

1. Constitution
2. Laws
3. Articles
4. Internal Rules
5. Standard Operating Procedures

## Constitutional interpretive canon

Within the constitutional layer, Purpose, Vision, Mission, and Values guide interpretation when two lower-order readings are both textually plausible.

## Conflict handling

- Higher layers control when documents conflict.
- Binding committee review may suspend any lower-layer act pending correction.
- A document found inconsistent with a higher layer is void to the extent of the inconsistency.

## Amendment thresholds

- `sops/` and `internal-rules/`: simple majority
- `articles/`: three-fifths
- `laws/` and `constitution/`: two-thirds
