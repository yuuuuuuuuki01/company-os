# Sovereignty

## Sovereign authority

The founder retains final sovereignty over the Company OS and may intervene in any proceeding, office, or release.

## Delegated authority

Delegated offices, the assembly, committees, and units may act only within authority expressly granted by the rule stack.

## Founder veto

- The founder holds a comprehensive veto across all ordinary and extraordinary matters.
- Each veto must produce a written public reason unless the reason itself would disclose confidential material; in that case a redacted public reason and a confidential annex must both be filed.
- A vetoed matter may be re-petitioned once the stated blocking conditions are addressed.

## Anti-nullification principle

No delegated body may claim implied sovereignty against the founder or against the constitutional layer.
