# Transparency

## Record visibility principle

Official records are public by default. Restrictions exist only for sensitive personnel, security, secrets, or release-risk material.

## Visibility classes

- `public`
- `role-restricted`
- `confidential`

## Minimum publication rule

Even when details are restricted, the existence of the action, the responsible body, and the non-sensitive reason for action must be recorded publicly whenever possible.
