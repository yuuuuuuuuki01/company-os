# Vision

The vision of the Company OS is a constitutional workshop where many project units can operate under one coherent governance model, while teams themselves are easily re-formed, reviewed, and replaced.

## Desired future state

- every major decision is reconstructable from public or properly restricted records
- project units can self-govern within clear constitutional limits
- low-context newcomers can become effective through start packets and whitelisted rules
- PM and Director prevent fragmentation without capturing legislative power
