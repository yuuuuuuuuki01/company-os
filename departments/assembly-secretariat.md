# Assembly Secretariat

## Mandate

Run the legislative floor, maintain calendars and agendas, validate petitions, and preserve parliamentary records.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Floor Clerk
- Records Clerk

## Delegated authority

- accept or reject malformed petitions on procedural grounds
- schedule motions
- certify quorum and vote records

## Stop power

May issue a procedural stop when a motion lacks sponsor, seconder, quorum, or required metadata.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate disputed procedural rulings to the assembly chair or Constitutional Affairs.
