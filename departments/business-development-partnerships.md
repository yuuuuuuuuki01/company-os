# Business Development and Partnerships

## Mandate

Manage partner outreach, strategic opportunities, external alignment, and non-final deal shaping.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Partnership Officer
- Business Development Lead

## Delegated authority

- run opportunity qualification
- draft partnership proposals
- coordinate cross-functional response for external opportunities

## Stop power

May place a hold on premature external commitments not cleared by Legal Policy, Finance Treasury, or Founder Office.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate all binding commitments to Founder Office and Legal Policy.
