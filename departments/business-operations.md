# Business Operations

## Mandate

Own operating cadence, cross-functional service operations, process health, and business rhythm design outside project-by-project delivery control.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Business Operations Lead
- Operations Analyst

## Delegated authority

- define recurring operating cadences
- maintain business process maps and service runbooks
- sponsor operating-efficiency reviews
- coordinate with PMO, Finance Treasury, and Customer Success on operational friction

## Stop power

May place an operational hold when recurring service work lacks an accountable process owner or stable runbook.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate systemic operating breakdowns to Founder Office, PMO, and Organizational Development and Learning.
