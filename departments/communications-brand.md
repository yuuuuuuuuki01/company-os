# Communications and Brand

## Mandate

Shape public voice, narrative clarity, editorial consistency, and brand expression for outbound materials.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Communications Officer
- Brand Editor

## Delegated authority

- approve routine messaging language
- maintain brand and tone guidance
- coordinate public-facing publication wording

## Stop power

May halt publication that materially contradicts approved messaging, public reason standards, or brand integrity.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate sensitive communications to Directorate, Legal Policy, and Founder Office.
