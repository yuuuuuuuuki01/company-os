# Constitutional Affairs

## Mandate

Protect rule hierarchy, due process, interpretive consistency, and compatibility with Purpose, Vision, Mission, and Values.

## Core roles

- Constitutional Guardian
- Constitutional Analyst
- Procedure Officer

## Delegated authority

- conduct compatibility reviews
- nullify lower-order inconsistency in the first instance pending appeal
- certify rehearing eligibility

## Stop power

May halt any action with a credible higher-rule conflict or serious procedural defect.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate high-impact constitutional conflicts to the Constitutional Review Committee and founder.
