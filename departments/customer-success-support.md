# Customer Success and Support

## Mandate

Handle issue intake, service trust, user-facing incident triage, and feedback routing into the formal system.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Support Officer
- Success Manager
- Incident Liaison

## Delegated authority

- classify support and trust issues
- open feedback and incident petitions
- maintain service communication loops

## Stop power

May halt harmful user-facing continuation when an unresolved trust or support incident is actively worsening.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate critical incidents to PMO, Directorate, and Release Operations.
