# Directorate

## Mandate

Guard coherence, meaning, quality bar, expressive alignment, and end-to-end conceptual consistency.

## Core roles

- Director
- Editorial Director
- Quality Direction Officer

## Delegated authority

- define quality standards across outputs
- require coherence corrections
- issue cross-unit quality guidance

## Stop power

May halt public or user-facing work when quality collapse or conceptual contradiction materially harms integrity.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate unresolved quality disputes to the assembly or Founder Office.
