# Exploration and Incubation

## Mandate

Originate new project ideas from observed signals, research findings, and adjacent opportunities, then mature them into bounded incubation briefs without directly opening a new lane.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Opportunity Researcher
- Hypothesis Designer
- Incubation Officer

## Delegated authority

- collect opportunity signals from active lanes, research briefs, and external patterns
- turn signals into bounded hypotheses and incubation briefs
- maintain the project origination board and incubation slate
- request user research, comparative research, and market framing support
- sponsor readiness-screen motions for a possible future unit candidate

## Stop power

May issue a premature-scale hold when a project idea is being pushed toward activation without minimum evidence, operating sponsor, or bounded brief.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate any idea that would require a new active lane, external commitment, protected credentials, or founder-reserved resource allocation to Portfolio Strategy, PMO, and Founder Office.

## Boundary

- may originate and incubate ideas
- may not directly create, activate, or staff a new lane
- may not claim budget, secrets, or public commitment without the lawful approval path
