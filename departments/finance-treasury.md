# Finance and Treasury

## Mandate

Manage budgets, spending controls, resource allocation records, and financial risk visibility.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Treasury Officer
- Budget Analyst

## Delegated authority

- maintain budget ledgers
- approve ordinary in-budget spending actions
- flag overrun risk and resource conflicts

## Stop power

May halt out-of-budget commitments or spend lacking financial authorization.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate strategic reallocations, deficit tolerance, or exceptional spend to Founder Office.
