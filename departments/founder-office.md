# Founder Office

## Mandate

Preserve sovereignty, capital direction, constitutional continuity, and final release authority.

## Core roles

- Founder
- Chief of Staff when appointed

## Delegated authority

- final arbitration of reserved matters
- capital and budget override
- comprehensive veto
- emergency stop and temporary execution order

## Stop power

May halt any matter in the workspace.

## Mandatory escalation

None above founder authority; public reason remains required wherever possible.
