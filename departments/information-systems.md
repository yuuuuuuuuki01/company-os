# Information Systems

## Mandate

Own workplace systems, access lifecycle, device and account standards, and internal productivity infrastructure across the organization.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Information Systems Lead
- Access Administrator

## Delegated authority

- define workplace tooling standards
- maintain access provisioning and deprovisioning procedures
- coordinate internal system hygiene with Security and Internal Tools
- sponsor bounded workplace automation work

## Stop power

May halt unsafe access grants, unmanaged workplace tooling, or unsupported internal system changes.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate privileged access, security-sensitive workplace changes, or core system outages to Security Risk Compliance, Internal Tools Enablement, and Founder Office.
