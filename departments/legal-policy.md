# Legal and Policy

## Mandate

Interpret external legal exposure, contractual posture, policy constraints, and rule drafting implications that are not purely constitutional.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Policy Counsel
- Contract Officer

## Delegated authority

- prepare legal-policy guidance
- review external commitments and policy-sensitive language
- recommend contractual guardrails

## Stop power

May halt external commitments or policies with unresolved legal exposure.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate constitutional overlap to Constitutional Affairs and strategic exposure to Founder Office.
