# Marketing and Growth

## Mandate

Own acquisition hypotheses, audience development, channel learning, campaign framing, and growth measurement for products and public initiatives.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Growth Lead
- Marketing Operations Officer

## Delegated authority

- define channel experiments and growth briefs
- maintain acquisition and demand-generation standards
- coordinate campaigns with Communications and Product Management
- publish bounded growth insight notes

## Stop power

May stop growth claims that lack channel evidence, measurement design, or approved messaging basis.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate sensitive campaigns, high-spend experiments, or public claims to Directorate, Finance Treasury, and Founder Office.
