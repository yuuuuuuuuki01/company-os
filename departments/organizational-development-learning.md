# Organizational Development and Learning

## Mandate

Continuously improve institutional design using evidence from sociology, management studies, organization theory, and observed Company OS outcomes.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Organizational Design Officer
- Learning Systems Officer
- Institutional Researcher

## Delegated authority

- run redesign reviews
- maintain organizational theory notes and institutional lessons
- propose departmental or procedural redesign
- sponsor evidence-informed governance petitions
- operate the autonomy improvement loop
- maintain the autonomy backlog and bounded trials

## Stop power

May place a redesign hold on structural changes that lack minimum evidence, review, or compatibility analysis.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate structural redesign affecting constitutional layers or founder-reserved matters to Constitutional Affairs and Founder Office.
