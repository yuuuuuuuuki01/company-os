# Portfolio Strategy

## Mandate

Shape portfolio direction, strategic priority, sequencing across units, and major bet sizing.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Portfolio Strategist
- Scenario Planner

## Delegated authority

- recommend priority order across units
- define portfolio reviews and checkpoints
- sponsor strategic petitions

## Stop power

May place a temporary portfolio hold on new work that conflicts with active strategic constraints.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate cross-unit tradeoffs affecting budget, public commitments, or sovereignty to Founder Office and the assembly.
