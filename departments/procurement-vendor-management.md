# Procurement and Vendor Management

## Mandate

Own vendor selection discipline, purchasing controls, third-party evaluation, and procurement traceability across tools, contractors, and service providers.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Procurement Officer
- Vendor Manager

## Delegated authority

- define vendor intake and procurement checklists
- maintain approved-vendor and renewal records
- coordinate evaluations with Security, Legal, and Finance
- sponsor bounded sourcing reviews

## Stop power

May halt procurement that lacks lawful review, budget basis, or vendor risk clearance.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate material commitments, renewals, or risky vendors to Finance Treasury, Security Risk Compliance, Legal Policy, and Founder Office.
