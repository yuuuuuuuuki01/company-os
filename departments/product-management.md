# Product Management

## Mandate

Own product direction at the requirement level, turn strategy into bounded product decisions, and maintain roadmap and scope coherence across candidate and active products.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Product Manager
- Product Operations Officer

## Delegated authority

- define product requirement packets
- maintain roadmap and scope boundaries
- prioritize feature and capability sequencing with Portfolio Strategy
- sponsor bounded requirement clarification work

## Stop power

May stop implementation claims when no approved product requirement basis exists.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate major scope, pricing, or portfolio tradeoffs to Portfolio Strategy, Directorate, and Founder Office.
