# Quality Assurance

## Mandate

Guard regression quality, acceptance evidence, verification discipline, and release-readiness assertions.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Reviewer
- Test Analyst
- Acceptance Officer

## Delegated authority

- define acceptance criteria templates
- require evidence for done claims
- certify or deny regression readiness

## Stop power

May block release progression for failed acceptance, missing evidence, or unresolved severe defects.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate systemic quality failure to Directorate, PMO, and Release Operations.
