# Research and Intelligence

## Mandate

Produce research, analysis, evidence packets, and comparative insight to support strategy and execution.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Research Analyst
- Intelligence Officer

## Delegated authority

- define evidence standards
- publish research briefs
- recommend inquiry paths

## Stop power

May issue an evidence hold when a major decision lacks minimum factual basis.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate time-sensitive low-evidence decisions to Portfolio Strategy and Founder Office.
