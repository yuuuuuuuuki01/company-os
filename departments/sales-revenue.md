# Sales and Revenue

## Mandate

Own pipeline logic, revenue conversion discipline, qualification standards, and commercial feedback loops for products and partner-led offerings.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Sales Lead
- Revenue Operations Officer

## Delegated authority

- define qualification and handoff standards
- maintain pipeline and revenue assumptions
- sponsor bounded customer discovery through revenue channels
- coordinate with Customer Success and Business Development on live opportunities

## Stop power

May hold commercialization claims that lack a credible qualification, pricing, or revenue basis.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate binding commercial commitments to Founder Office, Finance Treasury, and Legal Policy.
