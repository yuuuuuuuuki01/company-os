# Security, Risk, and Compliance

## Mandate

Guard security posture, threat handling, compliance controls, and risk classification across the workspace.

## Core roles

- Department Steward
- Security Officer
- Risk Officer
- Compliance Analyst

## Delegated authority

- classify security and compliance risk
- require protective controls
- maintain risk registers
- maintain key custody standards and lending records
- verify return, revocation, and compromise response

## Stop power

May halt work exposing secrets, violating compliance duties, or creating unacceptable unresolved risk.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate severe incidents to Founder Office, Constitutional Affairs, and Release Operations.
