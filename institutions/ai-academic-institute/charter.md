# AI Academic Institute Charter

## Mandate

Collect, review, and summarize up-to-date knowledge about AI systems, AI governance, AI safety, model capability shifts, tooling practices, and business-relevant uses of AI.

## Independence and limits

- the institute may not directly instruct departments, committees, or units
- the institute may not impose process changes by itself
- the institute may only influence the organization through reports, evidence appendices, and formally sponsored petitions

## Output classes

- weekly AI research bulletin
- topic memo
- structured recommendation report
- evidence appendix for strategy or governance motions

## Reporting standard

- reports must be in Japanese
- reports must be compressed and short by default
- direct recommendations must be clearly separated from observed facts

## Business use

Units may lawfully use institute outputs in product, strategy, operations, and governance, but adoption must occur through normal authority channels.
