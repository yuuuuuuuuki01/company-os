# AI Academic Institute Source Policy

## Preferred source order

1. primary research papers and official documentation
2. model cards, system cards, eval reports, and provider technical notes
3. recognized safety institutes and standards bodies
4. serious secondary analysis with explicit sourcing

## Reporting norm

Every bulletin must separate:

- what changed
- why it matters
- confidence level
- operational implication
- whether a formal petition is recommended
