# AI Bulletin Intake Rule

## Purpose

Turn AI bulletin updates into consistent operating intake.

## Classes

- `monitor`: keep tracking, no immediate change
- `trial`: route to ODL backlog review
- `petition`: route to assembly secretariat with a source summary

## First-pass ownership

Research Intelligence owns the first classification pass.

## Record duty

Classified items must be written to the AI bulletin intake board.
