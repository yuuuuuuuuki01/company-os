# Approval Record Rule

## Responsible department

`Data and Knowledge` owns approval records, approval indexes, and traceability of standing approvals.

## Record requirement

The following should be logged in the approval ledger:

- standing approvals
- exceptional approvals
- release approvals
- authority grants
- revocations

## Minimum fields

- date
- scope
- approver
- beneficiary
- duration or persistence
- evidence link
