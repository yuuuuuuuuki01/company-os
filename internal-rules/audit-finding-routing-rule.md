# Audit Finding Routing Rule

## Purpose

Turn audit findings into predictable operating paths.

## Classes

- `watch`: record and monitor
- `route`: open or attach to an improvement motion
- `urgent`: chair review before the next ordinary sitting

## Routing rule

- `watch` stays on the audit routing board
- `route` opens a draft motion or attaches to an active motion
- `urgent` must be surfaced to the chair and Founder Office immediately

## Record duty

All routed findings must appear in the audit routing board.
