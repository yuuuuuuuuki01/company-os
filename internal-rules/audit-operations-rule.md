# Audit Operations Rule

## Audit cadence

Operating audits should run on a standing cadence and additionally on founder request, constitutional trigger, or major incident.

## Minimum audit scope

- rule compliance
- role and authority compliance
- release control compliance
- record completeness
- context hygiene adherence
- response to prior findings

## Audit opinion classes

- regular
- regular with exceptions
- materially irregular
