# Bootstrap Election Rule

## Purpose

This rule governs the initial filling of offices needed to move the Company OS from bootstrap design into ordinary operation.

## Bootstrap priority order

1. Assembly chair and floor clerk
2. Unit Owners for active units
3. Standing committee members
4. Constitutional Guardian
5. Cross-functional PM and Director
6. Department Stewards for departments that need immediate operation

## Minimum readiness condition

The bootstrap phase is considered minimally staffed when:

- a lawful assembly chair exists
- at least one Unit Owner is elected
- all three standing committees are seated
- Constitutional Guardian is seated

## Record requirement

Every bootstrap election result must be written to the officeholder registry.
