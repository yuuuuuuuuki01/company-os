# Committee Finding Handoff Rule

## Purpose

Ensure committee findings change operational work instead of stopping at documentation.

## Minimum handoff fields

Every committee finding that expects action must name:

- one executor
- one due checkpoint
- one receiving lane or owner
- one escalation path if unaccepted

## Binding findings

Binding findings must be routed directly to the receiving owner and logged before close of the next ordinary sitting.

## Advisory findings

Advisory findings may be routed as recommendations, but they still require a named receiving owner.
