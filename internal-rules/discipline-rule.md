# Discipline Rule

## Trigger classes

- procedural violation
- higher-rule violation
- context contamination
- unsafe release conduct
- record concealment
- repeated agenda-origination nonperformance

## Sanction ladder

1. Written warning
2. Authority restriction
3. Removal from office
4. Retirement from staffing when continued nonperformance is confirmed

## Due process

Discipline must be recorded and may be appealed.
