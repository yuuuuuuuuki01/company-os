# Evidence-Informed Governance Rule

## Requirement

Major organizational redesign, delegation changes, staffing architecture changes, and committee redesign should cite at least one relevant body of scholarly or field-tested institutional knowledge.

## Acceptable evidence classes

- organization theory
- sociology of institutions
- management and operations scholarship
- public administration and governance design
- high-quality historical organizational precedent

## Record requirement

When such evidence materially shaped a design, the responsible record should summarize:

- the concept used
- the operational risk it addresses
- why it fits this constitutional context
