# Improvement Agenda Batching Rule

## Purpose

Allow the assembly to process multiple Company OS improvement items per sitting without re-asking for permission on every agenda item.

## Default batch size

- ordinary sittings should normally carry `3` to `7` agenda items
- at least `1` structural item should be included when structural debt exists
- at least `1` operating item should be included when active friction exists

## Chair authority

The chair may recognize and queue multiple related improvement motions in one sitting when:

- each item is recorded in the improvement board
- each item has an owner and next handoff
- no item bypasses higher-order approval requirements

## Priority bands

Agenda items should be grouped by:

1. `urgent`: blocking lawful operation, readability, or safety
2. `next`: high leverage improvements for current operation
3. `later`: useful but non-blocking improvements

## Safeguard

Batching changes meeting throughput only. It does not change voting thresholds, committee review duties, or founder-reserved powers.
