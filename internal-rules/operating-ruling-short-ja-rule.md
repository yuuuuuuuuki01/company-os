# Operating Ruling Short Japanese Rule

## Purpose

Keep routine rulings short in Japanese without losing the fields required for traceability.

## Initial scope

The short Japanese ruling pattern may be used for:

- ordinary sitting rulings
- committee operating rulings
- low-risk officer rulings tied to active work items

## Excluded scope

The full ruling template remains mandatory for:

- constitutional or law-level interpretation rulings
- founder veto records
- personnel discipline or removal rulings
- high-risk release or irreversible-action rulings

## Fallback rule

If the sponsor, chair, or reviewer believes compression could change legal meaning, the full ruling template must be used.

## Record duty

Every short ruling still preserves the same frontmatter fields as the full ruling template.
