# Reporting Standard Rule

## Default reporting language

All recurring institutional reports must be written in Japanese unless a higher-order record explicitly requires another language.

## Compression standard

Recurring reports should be as short as possible while preserving decision-useful signal.

## Required style

- short sections
- short bullets or short paragraphs
- no decorative framing
- no duplicated context
- clear separation of fact, opinion, and recommendation

## Applies to

- academic bulletins
- audit reports
- exception notices
- remediation summaries
- periodic governance reports
