# Research Intake Rule

## Scope

This rule governs intake of external scholarly and AI-related knowledge.

## Preferred source classes

- peer-reviewed papers
- reputable preprints with explicit caveats
- official model provider documentation
- safety institute outputs
- major lab system cards and evaluations
- serious industry benchmark and governance reports

## Intake record

Every research addition should log:

- source class
- date
- topic
- operational relevance
- adoption recommendation
- confidence and caveat note
