# Academic Interface Law

## Academic institution role

Academic institutions may gather external knowledge, summarize evidence, and issue formal research reports relevant to the Company OS and its businesses.

## Firewall rule

Academic institutions may not directly instruct, direct, stop, or reorganize departments, committees, or units.

## Allowed routes of influence

- research bulletin
- structured recommendation report
- petition sponsorship through lawful internal officers
- evidence appendices attached to redesign or strategy motions

## Business use

Business-facing units may use academic outputs as lawful inputs for design, strategy, and execution, provided the adoption happens through ordinary decision channels.
