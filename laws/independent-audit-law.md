# Independent Audit Law

## Audit role

The Audit Layer exists to assess whether the Company OS is operating according to its own rules, records, approvals, and controls.

## Independence principle

Auditors must not be line owners of the work they audit.

## Audit outputs

- periodic operating audit report
- control exception notice
- remediation recommendation
- audit opinion on operating regularity

## Audit limits

- auditors do not directly run the organization
- auditors do not amend rules by themselves
- auditors may trigger escalation, not direct management
