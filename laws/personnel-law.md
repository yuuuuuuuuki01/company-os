# Personnel Law

## Staffing model

Project teams are constituted per project and dissolved at project close unless a new petition recreates them.

## Election of major officers

- Major officers are elected by simple majority of the assembly.
- The assembly may use runoff voting when the chair determines that fragmented support prevents clear selection.
- Terms last through the project, subject to mid-project confidence review.

## Removal

Removal is grounded in rule violations or loss of confidence.

## Sanction ladder

1. Warning
2. Restriction of authority
3. Removal
