# Proposal Law

## Scope

This law governs petitions, motions, amendment handling, referral, postponement, and debate order.

## Minimum filing requirements

A proposal must include:

- a sponsor
- a seconder
- a target unit or cross-unit scope
- a requested action
- a rationale
- a risk classification
- a visibility classification

## Motion classes

- main motion
- amendment
- referral to committee
- postponement
- objection
- reconsideration

## Procedural order

Main motions are processed in the order recognized by the chair unless a higher-order emergency stop applies.
