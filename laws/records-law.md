# Records Law

## Public record default

Official records are public unless classified as `role-restricted` or `confidential`.

## Mandatory record classes

- proposals
- amendments
- objections
- votes
- rulings
- vetoes
- committee reports
- hiring ballots
- release decisions

## Minimum public disclosure

When a record is restricted, the public log should still reveal the existence of the act, the acting body, the unit, and a redacted statement of reason when safely possible.
