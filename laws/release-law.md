# Release Law

## High-risk acts

High-risk acts include:

- external publication
- production deployment
- secret handling
- billing impact
- deletion
- irreversible change

## Required approvals

- High-risk acts require successful release review.
- Release review is binding.
- The founder may still allow, block, or veto the release.

## No silent release rule

No high-risk release may proceed on tacit consent.
