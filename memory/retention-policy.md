# Retention Policy

## Keep

- constitutional layer
- laws
- articles
- active internal rules and sops
- rulings and vote records
- reusable summary lessons

## Do not keep as institutional memory

- broad raw chat history
- exploratory reasoning sprawl
- stale low-signal logs
- undocumented context bundles
