# Bootstrap Packet: Inaugural Assembly

## Goal

Move the Company OS from institutional design into initial staffed operation.

## Agenda

1. confirm quorum for inaugural session
2. elect assembly chair
3. elect floor clerk
4. elect initial Unit Owners
5. elect Personnel Committee
6. elect Constitutional Review Committee
7. elect Release Review Committee
8. elect Constitutional Guardian
9. elect PM
10. elect Director

## Success test

- inaugural session closes with recorded officeholders
- committee seats are filled
- at least one unit can lawfully vote
