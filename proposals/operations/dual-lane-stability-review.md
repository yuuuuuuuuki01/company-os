# Dual Lane Stability Review

## Goal

Verify that simultaneous `oem` and `shift` operation remains bounded, watchable, and free of immediate overload.

## Proposed action

- review the first checkpoint for both active external lanes
- confirm PMO, reviewer, approval, and seat continuity are intact
- name the next structural pressure without opening a third lane

## Why

- two active lanes change the operating burden of the company OS
- the next step should be evidence-based and bounded

## Discussion-1 note

- review should stay limited to the first dual-lane checkpoint
- accepted artifact set: `ledgers/oem-first-lane-watch-board.md`, `ledgers/shift-second-lane-watch-board.md`, and `ledgers/dual-lane-stability-board.md`
- discussion should identify only the next pressure, not a direct third activation

## Discussion-2 note

- the review should confirm dual-lane stability, preserve bounded operation, and open only a readiness screen for the next candidate
- no third lane may activate inside this review
- deliberation should decide whether the first dual-lane checkpoint is stable enough to proceed to screening

## Deliberation result

- adopted as the first dual-lane stability checkpoint
- `oem` and `shift` remain active under watch
- the next structural pressure is narrowed to `jouzou` readiness screening only
