# Exploration Incubation Department Expansion Motion

## Goal

Create a lawful department that can originate and incubate new project options without directly opening lanes.

## Proposed action

- establish `exploration-incubation` as a new department
- delegate bounded project origination and incubation authority
- add the project origination rule, SOP, and board as operating assets
- require all promoted ideas to use the existing readiness-screen and onboarding path

## Why

- Company OS can already operate multiple lanes and now needs a separate place to create future options
- origination should be productive without bypassing activation governance

## Deliberation result

- adopted as a department expansion with bounded authority only
- `exploration-incubation` may originate and incubate ideas, but may not activate lanes directly
