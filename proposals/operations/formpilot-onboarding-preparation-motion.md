# FormPilot Onboarding Preparation Motion

## Goal

Prepare the bounded onboarding bundle for `FormPilot` as the next candidate without opening a fourth active lane.

## Proposed action

- define the shortest lawful seat route for `FormPilot`
- draft the first bounded start packet for the outbound-sales automation MVP scope
- prepare reviewer and approval routes without activating the lane

## Why

- readiness screening shows `FormPilot` is viable but incomplete
- preparation-only work preserves the current triple-lane operating limit

## Discussion-1 note

- `FormPilot` should stay in preparation only while `oem`, `shift`, and `jouzou` remain the active lanes
- accepted artifact set: `ledgers/formpilot-readiness-screen-board.md`
- discussion should stay limited to one bounded preparation bundle

## Discussion-2 note

- the preparation bundle should include one seat route, one bounded start packet, and one concrete review/approval route
- `FormPilot` remains without a lane while `oem`, `shift`, and `jouzou` remain the active lanes
- accepted artifact set: `ledgers/formpilot-seat-route-board.md`, `recruiting/formpilot-unit-owner-start-packet.md`, and `ledgers/formpilot-review-approval-route.md`
- deliberation should decide whether the bounded `FormPilot` preparation bundle is sufficient to proceed

## Deliberation result

- adopted as the bounded `FormPilot` preparation bundle
- `FormPilot` remains unseated and does not enter activation in this motion
- supporting artifacts: `ledgers/formpilot-seat-route-board.md`, `recruiting/formpilot-unit-owner-start-packet.md`, and `ledgers/formpilot-review-approval-route.md`
