# Organizational Subdivision V1 Motion

## Goal

Allow Company OS to scale below the department layer by adding lawful divisions and sections with explicit delegation records.

## Proposed action

- adopt the department -> division -> section layer model
- add the organizational scaling rule and SOP
- create a subdivision registry and delegation register
- record the first approved divisions and sections for research, incubation, and lane watch work

## Why

- concurrent operation has outgrown a department-only model
- finer delegation is needed without losing traceability or constitutional control

## Deliberation result

- adopted as the first subdivision package
- scaling must remain explicit, reviewable, and reversible
