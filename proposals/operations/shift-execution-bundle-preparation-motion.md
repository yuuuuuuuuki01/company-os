# Shift Execution Bundle Preparation Motion

## Goal

Prepare the smallest lawful execution bundle for `shift` after the execution threshold has been adopted.

## Proposed action

- prepare one bounded seat-execution step for the initial `shift` unit-owner line
- prepare one bounded reviewer-confirmation step
- prepare one bounded approval and rollback note for a later execution motion

## Why

- threshold adoption should lead to reusable execution artifacts, not immediate activation
- the next lawful step is preparation of a bounded execution bundle only

## Discussion-1 note

- this motion should prepare an execution bundle only and should not activate `shift`
- accepted artifact set: `ledgers/shift-execution-threshold-board.md`
- discussion should stay limited to the smallest repeatable execution bundle

## Discussion-2 note

- the execution bundle should name one seat-execution motion, one reviewer-confirmation step, one approval-path acceptance step, and one rollback note
- `shift` remains `ready` until a separate execution motion is adopted
- accepted artifact set: `ledgers/shift-execution-bundle-board.md` and `proposals/operations/shift-unit-owner-seating-motion.md`
- deliberation should decide whether the smallest lawful execution bundle is sufficient to proceed

## Deliberation result

- adopted as the smallest lawful execution bundle for `shift`
- `shift` remains `ready` and does not enter activation in this motion
- supporting artifacts: `ledgers/shift-execution-bundle-board.md` and `proposals/operations/shift-unit-owner-seating-motion.md`
