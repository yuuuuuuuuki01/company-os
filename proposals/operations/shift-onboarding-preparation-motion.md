# Shift Onboarding Preparation Motion

## Goal

Prepare the bounded onboarding bundle for `shift` as the second external candidate while `oem` runs in watch mode.

## Proposed action

- define the shortest lawful seat route for `shift`
- draft the first bounded start packet for the MVP planning scope
- prepare reviewer and approval routes without activating the lane

## Why

- `shift` is the standing fallback and natural second candidate
- the company OS should prove that onboarding is repeatable across more than one unit

## Discussion-1 note

- `shift` should stay in preparation only while `oem` remains the active first lane
- accepted artifact set: `ledgers/shift-onboarding-preparation-board.md`
- send to `discussion-2` with one bounded preparation bundle only

## Discussion-2 note

- the preparation bundle should include one seat route, one bounded start packet, and one concrete review/approval route
- `shift` remains `ready` while `oem` is the active first lane
- accepted artifact set: `ledgers/shift-seat-route-board.md`, `recruiting/shift-unit-owner-start-packet.md`, and `ledgers/shift-review-approval-route.md`
- deliberation should decide whether the bounded `shift` preparation bundle is sufficient to proceed

## Deliberation result

- adopted as the bounded `shift` preparation bundle
- `shift` remains `ready` and does not enter execution in this motion
- supporting artifacts: `ledgers/shift-seat-route-board.md`, `recruiting/shift-unit-owner-start-packet.md`, and `ledgers/shift-review-approval-route.md`
