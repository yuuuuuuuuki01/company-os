# Standard Organization Package Motion

## Goal

Fill the missing departments needed for a standard operating company structure without disturbing the existing constitutional operating model.

## Proposed action

- add Product Management, Sales and Revenue, Marketing and Growth, Business Operations, Procurement and Vendor Management, and Information Systems
- place each new department into the registry, delegation matrix, and work-allocation board
- assign bounded initial work and escalation routes to each new department

## Why

- Company OS already covers governance and execution well, but still lacks several standard business functions
- adding the missing functions now makes later project growth more realistic and less founder-dependent

## Deliberation result

- adopted as the standard organization package
- new departments become active but start with bounded initial scopes
