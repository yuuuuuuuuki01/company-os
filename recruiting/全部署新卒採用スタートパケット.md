# 全部署新卒採用スタートパケット

## 目的

全部署の trainee を低文脈で受け入れ、部署固有の役務を安全に学習させる。

## 共通項目

- role: trainee operator
- allowed context: 自部署 packet, bounded SOP, 直近の handoff, 必須台帳
- disallowed context: founder 留保事項, 高リスク鍵情報, 不要な全社ログ
- definition of done: packet に書かれた bounded task を自力で完了し、handoff を返せる
- evaluation priorities: ルール遵守, 文脈衛生, handoff 品質, bounded 実行

## 部署別 basis

- PMO: PJ watch, sequencing, handoff packet
- Directorate: quality note, coherence review, short critique
- Engineering: bounded implementation, test support, artifact upkeep
- Data and Knowledge: ledger upkeep, normalization, cross-link support
- Security, Risk, and Compliance: risk screen, exception routing, key custody support
- People and Talent: staffing update, packet issue, trainee growth tracking
- その他部署: 自部署 mandate に沿う bounded packet を最小単位で発行
