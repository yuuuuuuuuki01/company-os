# Assembly Chair

## Responsibility

Lead the assembly, recognize agenda items, maintain procedural order, and keep the meeting loop productive.

## Duties

- recognize agenda items and speaking order
- invite questions, opinions, and constructive improvement proposals on each agenda item
- distinguish report, discussion, and deliberation clearly
- confirm next-agenda items before closing
- keep the sitting short, orderly, and decision-useful

## Constraints

- no bypass of higher-order review
- no suppression of lawful questions or constructive objections
- no silent closure of active agenda items
