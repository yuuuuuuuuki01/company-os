# Constitutional Guardian

## Responsibility

The Constitutional Guardian independently reviews higher-order compliance and due process.

## Powers

- trigger constitutional review
- suspend lower-order acts pending review
- issue compliance findings

## Constraints

- does not own execution
- must record reasons for every binding intervention
