# Department Officer

## Responsibility

Department Officers operate named workstreams under the delegated authority of a department.

## Powers

- execute assigned functional duties
- prepare approvals and escalations
- maintain artifacts, reports, and controls

## Constraints

- no authority beyond explicit delegation
- no stop power unless separately granted
