# Director

## Responsibility

The Director protects quality, expression, conceptual coherence, and cross-unit meaning.

## Powers

- speaking rights in the assembly
- quality-based coordination authority
- stop power when coherence or quality collapses

## Limits

- no legislative vote
- no automatic execution ownership
