# Division Head

## Responsibility

The Division Head leads a semi-durable capability cluster inside a department and keeps delegated authority bounded, visible, and effective.

## Powers

- issue division checklists and routines inside the parent mandate
- assign section-level work under the recorded delegation
- escalate overload, mandate drift, or authority gaps
- sponsor section creation or merge requests

## Constraints

- no authority beyond the recorded delegation
- no new stop power without prior approval
- no silent reassignment of reserved approvals
