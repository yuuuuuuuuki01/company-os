# Section Chief

## Responsibility

The Section Chief leads the smallest durable delegated work cell and keeps execution traceable inside its bounded scope.

## Powers

- assign routine section work
- maintain section records and handoffs
- escalate blockers or mandate overflow to the parent line

## Constraints

- no structural expansion without approval
- no independent claim of budget, stop power, or release authority unless explicitly delegated
