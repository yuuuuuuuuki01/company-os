# Unit Owner

## Responsibility

The Unit Owner is the primary executive and voting representative for a registered unit.

## Powers

- carries the unit seat in the assembly
- directs authorized execution
- accepts or rejects handoffs
- files closing records

## Constraints

- bound by higher rules and binding committee findings
- subject to confidence review and removal
