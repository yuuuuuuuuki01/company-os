# SOP: Publish Research Bulletin

1. Collect candidate sources from allowed source classes.
2. Summarize the newest material with explicit caveats.
3. Separate observations from recommendations.
4. File the bulletin in `institutions/ai-academic-institute/reports/`.
5. If a design or governance change is recommended, route it through a formal petition.
