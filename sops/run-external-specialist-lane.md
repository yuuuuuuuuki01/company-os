# SOP: Run External Specialist Lane

1. Name the artifact to be delegated and keep scope narrow.
2. Assign the sponsoring department.
3. Assign the internal reviewer and the final acceptance owner.
4. Write a short brief with goal, constraints, and definition of done.
5. Add the lane to the external specialist board.
6. Receive the external output without granting it governance authority.
7. Review internally for fit, safety, and traceability.
8. Accept, revise, or reject the result.
9. Record the outcome and next handoff.
