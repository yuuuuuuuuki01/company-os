# Run Organization Scaling Loop

## Goal

Detect when Company OS needs more structure and convert that need into lawful departments, divisions, or sections.

## Steps

1. review recurring overload, approval delay, and cross-domain confusion from active ledgers
2. decide whether the smallest sufficient response is a section, a division, or a new department
3. draft a scaling request with parent line, mandate, delegated authority, and rollback condition
4. log the request in `ledgers/organizational-scaling-board.md`
5. mirror structural requests in `ledgers/department-growth-board.md`
6. route compatibility review through Constitutional Affairs and affected control departments
7. after approval, add the new line to `ledgers/subdivision-registry.md` and `ledgers/delegation-register.md`
8. assign the next confidence review point

## Output rule

- reports stay short and in Japanese when routed into ordinary operations
- no new structural line becomes active before its delegation is recorded
